Kobe Bryant

A tribute

Kobe Bryant, celebrating after winning his fifth championship

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

  • 1992 - Began his high school career at Lower Merion High School in Philadelphia and played on the varsity basketball team as a freshman

  • 1995 - Named Pennsylvania Player of the Year in his junior year

  • 1996 - Leads his team to their first state championship in 53 years in his senior year. Gets drafted to the NBA to the Los Angeles Lakers.

  • 1997 - Becomes the youngest to win the Slam Dunk Contest at the age of 18

  • 1999-2002 - Wins 3 NBA Championships in a row

  • 2006 - Scores a career high 81 points against the Toronto Raptors, which is the second-highest point total in NBA history. Wins NBA Scoring Champgion award

  • 2007 - Wins back to back NBA Scoring Champion award

  • 2008 - Wins NBA Most Valuable Player award and wins a gold medal for Team USA at the Olympics

  • 2009-2010 - Wins back to back NBA championships and NBA Finals MVP awards

  • 2012 - Wins another gold medal for Team USA at the Olympics

  • 2016 - In his final NBA season and game, Kobe scores 60 points against the Utah Jazz at 37 years and 234 days old, becoming the oldest player to score 60 points

  • 2018 - Wins an Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for his film 'Dear Basketball'. Becomes first African-American and first former professional athlete to win.

  • 2020 - Passed away in a tragic helicopter crash on January 26 at the age of 41
